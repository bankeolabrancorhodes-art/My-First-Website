Banks Recipe: Fried Yams and Egg Sauce

We will be making the popular Nigerian Fried yam and Egg sauce for Sunday lunch.

Ingredients: >
  • Thinly sliced yams
  • 4 Large fresh eggs ( Ensure to test for freshness before breaking)
  • 1 chopped onions
  • 1tbspn minced garlic
  • 1 teaspn ginger
  • 1 teaspn ground pepper
  • 1 cup chopped spinach
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1 cup minced meat
  • 1tbspn butter
  • salt
  • tumeric

    • Cooking Process: 25 minutes

    1. Fry yam on all sides for 5 minutes or until crispy and golden in color
    2. Put butter in preheated pan add onions, garlic, ginger and simmer on low heat for 2 minutes
    3. Add minced meat and continue to cook on low heat for 5 to 10 minutes or until all juices form the meat evaporates
    4. Add tomatoes and pepper and cover as it simmers for another 5 minutes
    5. Add spinach, then lightly beaten eggs and stir continuously for 3 minutes
    6. Add salt to taste

    Note: Yams here refer to either the African white yams or the regular potatoes
